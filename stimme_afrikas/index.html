Upcoming Events

Giessen Music Festival: "Voices of Africa"

Date: June 15, 2024

Location: Stadttheater Gießen

Join us for a vibrant evening at the renowned Stadttheater Gießen as we participate in the Giessen Music Festival. Our "Voices of Africa" performance will feature a unique blend of traditional African songs and contemporary compositions.

Choral Workshop and Public Performance: "Harmony in Diversity"

Date: April 17-24, 2024

Location: Kongresshalle Gießen

Inviting choral enthusiasts to a three-day workshop focusing on African choral music, culminating in a public performance at the Kongresshalle Gießen.

Summer Open-Air Concert: "Melodies Under the Stars"

Date: August 21, 2024

Location: Gießener Seewiese

Celebrate the longest day of the year with us at our Summer Open-Air Concert, "Melodies Under the Stars," at Gießener Seewiese. Enjoy a selection of uplifting African and international choral music.
